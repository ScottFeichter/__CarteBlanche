Carte Blanche was founded in 2024 by vocalist Laurent Fourgo and guitarist Scott Feichter.

The name describes the concept - anything is possible; we can play whatever songs we wish.

We also love to entertain an audience and therefore try to accomodate audience requests.

With boundaries, our music is oriented around the greats: Sinatra, Elvis, Gainsbourg, Bowie, Dutronc.

Our configuration adapts to suit your space and your budget - visit the links right to learn more.

We love to have fun and if you have fun we love it even more! See you soon - au revoir.
